About Us

Enjoy Scrumptious, Authentic Indian Dishes

Prepared and served with the utmost care, We bring you a delightful array of aromatic dishes handcrafted and perfected by our chef. All restaurant items can be enjoyed in the warm, comfortable atmosphere of our Indian Restaurant in Dehradun, Uttrakhand, or you can order take-out.
